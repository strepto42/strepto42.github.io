Jezebel

You are here: Home > Photography > Jezebel

We have a new puppy. Her name is Jezebel. She is, of course, extremely cute.

I've always wanted to get search engine hits for "pictures of puppies", just to pollute the Internet, so here goes. I'll try not to upload too many "baby photos" here, but the cutest simply must be shared with the world.

Like typical doting parents, we've put her on puppywar.com, and of course with a picture like that she should bloody well win, but there's no accounting for taste.

Update: She's now old enough to get out and visit the local dog park. Much crazed wrestling and chasing of tennis balls is the order of the day. She's also becoming somewhat less naughty as she gets older - although she is clearly cabable of still being an extremely naughty puppy at times.
