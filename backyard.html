Backyard Creatures

You are here: Home > Photography > Backyard Creatures

One of the nice things about living in Australia is that you get visited by various creatures, even in the city. Here are some pictures of various visitors and residents of our back yard, including a nice big Blue-tongue Lizard, and a White-faced Heron.

There are also pics of a massive grasshopper (which may have been eaten now, as I haven't seen it for a while, but it was hanging around for months), fighting Geckos, a few miscellaneous pictures of one of the local hospital's Apocalypsenowmobiles, my first IR picture (more here), and some random shots of the pink Frangipani in a particularly nice evening light.
