Guy and Alison's Wedding

You are here: Home > Photography > Guy and Alison's Wedding

Here are my pictures from Guy and Alison's wedding. They were kind enough to ask me to be their official photographer, but I declined, suggesting instead that they get someone who knew what they were doing (as I've never shot a wedding in my life).

I think it was a sensible decision, but I'm still quite happy with some of these shots... and they make such a pretty couple.

I could wax lyrical here about weddings, the characters involved, and photography in general, but instead I might just let the pictures speak for themselves.

All the best for your future, guys!
