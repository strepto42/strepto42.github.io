Greetings from the Other Side!

Hello all!

Well, I'm here, finally made it. What a place... the chaos is unbelievable, but somehow organised, and people manage. Driving here would be an experience; lanes are pretty much optional, people MOSTLY drive on the correct side of the road, and beeping your horn at anything and everything is the norm, including cows of course, of which there are many.

Not much else to report yet of course; temp here is a balmy 25-30, pollution here is unbeliveable, think it'll be better once we get out of Delhi.

Will send more when I have more to send... adios for now!
