Naima's 28th birthday dinner

Mim has quickly grown to 28 (happy birthday!) and so a dinner and drinks were called for. Much merriment took place afterwards, including an impromteau organ jam including such favourites as The Girl From Ipanema, and an air guitar session to accompany some classic Bodycount. The pictures speak for themselves.
