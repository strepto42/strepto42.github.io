Ever since I travelled around Australia in 2005, I've become quite interested in photography of all sorts.

This includes landscapes, portraits and (thanks to Jana) birds. I also do professional product photography for my reviews on Dan's Data, martial art photography for Pacific International Taekwondo, and the odd wedding for family and friends.

Managing an ever-increasing picture load manually on this site became an enormous chore though, so now all my photos go up on to flickr.com. Click here to check them out.

On this site you can still find a selection of random pictures from over the years though... enjoy!
