Fuzzy Polaroid

You are here: Home > Photography > Fuzzy Polaroid

Fuzzy Polaroid is the latest musical endeavour that I've been roped into (and my debut as a drummer).

We play a funky blues rock set of Nathan's originals. Thus far we've had two whole gigs, and these are pictures from my camera which was present at both. Thanks heaps to Pete and Ben for taking them!

The music we played at these two gigs also got recorded - for mp3s, click here.
