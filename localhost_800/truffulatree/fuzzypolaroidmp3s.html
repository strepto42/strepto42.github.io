Fuzzy Polaroid

You are here: Home > Music > Fuzzy Polaroid


Fuzzy Polaroid is the latest musical endeavour that I've been roped into (and my debut as a drummer).

We play a funky blues rock set of Nathan's originals. Thus far we've had two whole gigs, and these are the MP3s from my little recorder which was present at both.

It is left as an exercise to the listener to decide which version of each song they like better. I could tell you - but that would spoil the fun of making you listen to both.

For pictures from the gigs, click here.

Nathan has also created a Myspace page for the band, although it's really just demos at this stage. We'll record proper versions of the songs in due course I think (because that'll be FUN).
