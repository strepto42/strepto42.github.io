0x0021st Birthday

You are here: Home > Photography > 0x0021st Birthday

Another year, another birthday. Except, this year I turned 0x0021 - that's 33 in base 16, for those of you not blessed with an intimate knowledge of hex.

A fun time was had by all, chatting, drinking, and jamming the night away. In keeping with the nerdly theme of the evening, we had our very first drum, bass and soundboard jam - and I managed to record a fair bit of it, so keep an eye on the Consistent User Experience page for some hilariously bad new music.

In the meantime, here is the modest collection of photos from the night. Even though is was a modest occasion, I got carried away and made an extremely disturbing invitation, and I feel it should be shared with the world, scary as it is, so I've included it too.
