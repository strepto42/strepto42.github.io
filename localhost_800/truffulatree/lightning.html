Thunderstorm Moonrise

You are here: Home > Photography > Thunderstorm Moonrise

Lightning Crashes - at least, if you're close enough to hear it. This lovely storm was about 80km away out over the sea, so I didn't hear a thing, but it was a pretty bloody spectacular light show.

The moon rising above the clouds was the icing on the cake. I love it when nature turns on this sort of spectacle, and I especially love it when I have a perfect view from 20m down my street. :)
