Cunningham's Gap

You are here: Home > Photography > Cunningham's Gap

Ben, Moz and I spent a very relaxing day in the bush exploring Cunningham's Gap, about an hour southwest of Brisbane. The birds proved to be quite elusive but we still got a few nice shots, including some Bell Miners, Scrub Wrens and Thornbills (I think).
